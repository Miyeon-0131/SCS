Where the Proceeds Go? 收益去向
Our efforts are dedicated to supporting others. After covering the essential expenses required to operate the shop, 100% of our proceeds are donated to meaningful causes, ensuring that every purchase helps create a positive impact beyond our school community. 我们的努力旨在支持他人。收益中的一部分用于保证商店基本运行，剩下的所有收益100%将捐赠给有意义的公益事业，确保每一笔消费都能为我们学校社区以外的社会产生积极影响。
Every six months, we will publicly disclose the income and expenditure records on our website, along with the allocation of the proceeds. Once our partner organization is confirmed, the name of the charity will be announced on the website. 每半年我们将在网站上公布半年的收支记录，以及所得收益的去向。合作机构确定后，我们会将机构名称在网站上公布。
How to Buy? 如何购买
This website only supports pre-orders, with transactions occurring every Friday at noon during the semester. The product redemption booth is located on the 1st floor of the Domestic Division High School and Middle School Building and is open every Friday from 12:30 to 12:55, accepting cash, WeChat Pay, and Alipay. 本网站仅支持预订，交易时间为学期中每周五中午。产品兑换柜台位于中国部中学教学楼1楼，开放时间为每周五12:30到12:55，接受现金、微信支付和支付宝。
All pre-ordered items will be reserved for two weeks. If unclaimed within this period, the order will be automatically canceled. For special circumstances, please contact us. Purchases are limited to current students and staff; account registration requires a school email. 所有预订商品将保留两周。如果在此期限内未领取，订单将自动取消。如有特殊情况，请联系我们。购买仅限在校学生和教职人员。预订需使用学校邮箱。
* All products are currently pre-sale and can be pre-ordered on the official website. The first transaction will take place on December 27th, the Winter Solstice Festival and all clothing products will be delivered in January. 所有产品均在预售阶段，可在官网先行预定。首次交易将于12月27日冬至日进行，所有服装类产品一月交付。
